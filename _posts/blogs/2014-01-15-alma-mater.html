University of Pennsylvania (2013-15)

MSc. Robotics


I completed my master's in Robotics at the University of Pennsylvania. I had a brilliant time at Penn learning from some of the best in Robotics. After long contemplation at the end of my degree, I decided to focus on machine learning which gave me the most excitement within robotics.



National Institute of Technology, Trichy, India (2009-13)

B.Tech Mechanical Engineering


I completed my bachelors (B.Tech) at the National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli (NITT) in Mechanical Engineering. NITT was and still is ranked amongst the top fifteen schools for engineering in India, with a challenging entrance procedure (AIEEE). I found Robotics here and the courses equipped me with tools to pursue my master's in my field in it. NITT burst with life; with the innumerable clubs and activities, engineering is only one of the many things NITT offered.
